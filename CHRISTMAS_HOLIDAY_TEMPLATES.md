## Usage Tips

### SMS Best Practices

1. **Keep it under 160 characters** when possible to ensure single message
2. **Use emojis strategically** - they grab attention but don't overdo it
3. **Personalize with first name** - increases open and engagement rates
4. **Send between 10 AM - 8 PM** local time
5. **Test variables** before sending to ensure proper replacement

### Email Best Practices

1. **Test on mobile devices** - 60%+ of emails are opened on mobile
2. **Preview text matters** - Write compelling preheaders
3. **Subject lines:** Keep under 50 characters for mobile
4. **Timing:** Send early December for Christmas, late December for New Year
5. **A/B test:** Try different subject lines to see what works best

### Personalization Variables

**Required Variables:**

- `{{first_name}}` - Customer's first name
- `{{company_name}}` - Your business name

**Optional Variables:**

- `{{discount}}` - Discount percentage (e.g., 25, 30, 50)
- `{{link}}` or `{{shop_url}}` - Website/product URL
- `{{code}}` - Promo code (e.g., XMAS2025)
- `{{company_address}}` - Physical address for footer
- `{{support_email}}` - Customer support email
- `{{support_phone}}` - Customer support phone

### Recommended Sending Schedule

**Christmas Campaign:**

- **First Send:** December 20-22 (Pure gratitude message)
- **Second Send:** December 23-24 (Last-minute shopping offer)
- **Final Send:** December 25 (Christmas day greetings)

**New Year Campaign:**

- **First Send:** December 29-30 (Year-end thank you)
- **Second Send:** December 31 (New Year's Eve wishes)
- **Third Send:** January 1 (Fresh start message)

### Rate Limiting Note

Remember: The system limits **1 SMS per customer per day** to prevent spam. Plan your campaign schedule accordingly!
